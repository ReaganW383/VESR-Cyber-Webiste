The Sony PlayStation buisness is one with mutiple avenues, but primarily

What is my role at PlayStation?

As a software developer, I design, develop, and maintain software for PlayStations
consoles, services, and apps which can range from upkeeping system software to
creating game development tools for cloud infrastructure and mobile applications.

Ethical Issues?

  • Some ethical issues that could arise with being a software developer is in game design and user experience
    more noteably with addictive design styles that can manipulate players and monetization.
  • Another example of this is in Data and privacy design through Data collection and use
    in that developers must ensure consent is obtained and that data is obtained securely
    to prevent breaches

    • Cybersecurity Issues Arise

  • Confidentiality: PlayStation must ethically protect the vast amount of personal information
    it holds, including names, addresses, and purchase history.
  • Encrytion v.s. security: Even if credit card data is encryted, other personally identifiable
    information (PII) can still be exposed, creating ethical questions about the overall
    effectiveness of security measures.

    • Best Practices with Cybersecurity!

    1. Strong Authentication
    2. Account Privacy
    3. Unique, Strong Passwords
    4. Offical Sources Only
    5. Beware of Social Engineering
