My Personal Values

We all have those things we care about deep down — the stuff that helps us make decisions, stay grounded, and not totally lose it when things get messy. Around here, our personal values are kind of like our internal compass (minus the cheesy quotes).
They're not about being perfect — just about showing up, being real, and doing things in a way that feels right. Wanna know what keeps us moving? Keep reading.

My Values

Honesty

  1. Upholding truthfulness and transparency in all communications and actions
  2. Demonstrating integrity by being sincere and straightforward
  3. Building strong trust through candidness and ethical behavior
  4. Courageously admitting errors or shortcomings without deflection
  5. Fostering an environment where openness and authenticity are valued
  6. Avoiding deceit, manipulation, or withholding critical information
  7. Devolped through life experiences and realizing that no matter what, how you really feel
    always comes to light

Consistency

  1. Exhibiting unwavering reliability and steadiness across time and circumstances
  2. Maintaining uniform standards of behavior, performance, and decision-making
  3. Building confidence and trustworthiness through predictability and dependability
  4. Creating and adhering to routines and habits that reinforce positive outcomes
  5. Delivering continuous quality and effort regardless of external pressures
  6. Strengthening relationships by reliably meeting or exceeding expectations
  7. Developed through the feeling I would recieve after improving in an activity like drawing
    that I would do daily in order to get better

Curiosity

  1. Cultivating a deep and genuine desire to explore, learn, and discover new knowledge
  2. Actively questioning assumptions and seeking diverse perspectives
  3. Embracing intellectual openness and a willingness to challenge the status quo
  4. Engaging in lifelong learning and self-improvement
  5. Harnessing inquisitiveness to innovate and solve problems creatively
  6. Remaining mentally flexible and adaptable to change
  7. Using curiosity as a driver for personal and professional growth

Dependibility

  1. Being consistently reliable in fulfilling responsibilities and commitments
  2. Demonstrating punctuality, accountability, and conscientiousness
  3. Following through on tasks without needing supervision or reminders
  4. Serving as a dependable pillar of support for colleagues, friends, and family
  5. Building a reputation for trustworthiness and solid performance under pressure
  6. Taking ownership and responsibility for outcomes, both good and bad
  7. Acting with integrity and consistency to foster confidence in others
